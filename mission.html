I served in the Missouri St. Louis Mission. Here are some fun facts about my mission.

Places I served in:
  1. O’Fallon MO West
    • Lake St. Louis Stake
    • Fast-growing suburban area outside of St. Louis
    • Similar to Utah’s landscape, but more green and a little more rural
  2. Rantoul
    • Champaign Stake
    • Home to the abandoned Chanute Air Force Base
    • Northernmost area of our mission
  3. Mount Vernon
    • O'Fallon IL Stake
    • A truck stop town with plenty of great places to eat in the area
    • Home of Rend Lake
  4. Warrenton
    • Lake St. Louis Stake
    • A mix of both suburb and rural outside the St. Louis megalopolis
    • Half hour west of O’Fallon, Missouri
  5. Fulton
    • Columbia Stake
    • Small college town home to both Westminster College, William Woods University, and the Missouri School for the Deaf
    • Many local restaurants and businesses
  6. Rockwood 1st
    • St. Louis Stake
    • The richest neighborhoods and shopping district of St. Louis
    • Covers part of Wildwood and part of Chesterfield

Fun fact: The owner of the Jacksonville Jaguars, Shad Khan, grew up near Rantoul, and actually owns farmland in a lot of the surrounding counties. Check it out!
